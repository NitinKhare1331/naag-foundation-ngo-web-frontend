Welcome to NAAG Foundation Trust
Stablished Since 1995

Women Empowerment: We work to break the barriers holding women back by promoting education, skill development, and economic independence. Our initiatives aim to create a safe and supportive environment where women can thrive and lead.

Child Welfare: Every child deserves a childhood full of love, care, and opportunity. We strive to protect, nurture, and educate childrenâ€”especially those from underprivileged backgroundsâ€”so they can grow into confident, capable individuals.

Support for Persons with Disabilities: We advocate for the rights and dignity of individuals with disabilities, offering programs that promote accessibility, vocational training, and social integration to ensure they lead fulfilling and independent lives.

Environmental Protection: We believe in a sustainable future. Through tree plantations, clean-up drives, and environmental awareness campaigns, we actively engage communities in preserving and protecting our planet.

At NAAG Foundation Trust, we believe that real change begins at the grassroots level and through collective action, we're building a brighter, more inclusive future for all.
